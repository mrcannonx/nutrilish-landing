Before You Shop
  • Check what's already in fridge & pantry
  • Plan 3–5 meals for the week
  • Set a budget ($50–$100/person/week)
  • Eat before shopping (never shop hungry!)
  • Bring reusable bags
Pro Tip

Buy proteins in bulk when on sale. Freeze in individual portions for up to 3 months.
