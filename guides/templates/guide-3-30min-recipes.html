🍯 1. Honey Garlic Chicken + Rice

⏱ Prep: 5 min 🍳 Cook: 25 min 🍽 Servings: 4
485
Cal
42g
Protein
48g
Carbs
12g
Fat

Ingredients

  • 1.5 lb chicken breast
  • 3 tbsp honey
  • 3 tbsp soy sauce
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 cups jasmine rice
  • Salt & pepper
  • Sesame seeds

Instructions

  1. Cook rice per package directions.
  2. Cut chicken into strips, season with salt and pepper.
  3. Heat oil in skillet, cook chicken 5–6 min per side.
  4. Mix honey + soy sauce + garlic, pour over chicken.
  5. Simmer 2 min until glazed.
  6. Serve over rice, top with sesame seeds.

Ingredients

  • 4 salmon fillets (6oz each)
  • 2 lemons
  • 3 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 tsp Italian herbs
  • 1 lb asparagus
  • 2 cups couscous
  • Salt & pepper

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 400°F.
  2. Place salmon and asparagus on a sheet pan.

  4. Bake 12–15 min until salmon flakes easily.
  5. Cook couscous per package directions.
  6. Plate and portion into 4 containers.

Ingredients

  • 1.5 lb flank steak, sliced thin
  • 4 cups broccoli florets
  • 3 tbsp soy sauce
  • 2 tbsp oyster sauce
  • 1 tbsp sesame oil
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 1 tbsp cornstarch
  • 2 cups white rice (cooked)

Instructions

  1. Cook rice. Mix soy + oyster sauce + cornstarch + 2 tbsp water.
  2. Sear beef in hot wok 2 min, remove.
  3. Stir-fry broccoli + garlic 3 min.
  4. Return beef, pour sauce over top.
  5. Simmer until sauce thickens, 2 min. Serve over rice.

🍳 20. Protein-Packed Fried Rice

⏱ Prep: 5 min 🍳 Cook: 15 min 🍽 Servings: 4
460
Cal
36g
Protein
44g
Carbs
14g
Fat

Ingredients

  • 3 cups cooked rice (cold)
  • 1 lb chicken breast, diced
  • 4 eggs
  • 1 cup frozen mixed veggies
  • 3 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp sesame oil
  • Green onions, garlic

Instructions

  1. Scramble eggs in wok, set aside.
  2. Cook diced chicken 5–6 min until done, remove.
  3. Stir-fry frozen veggies + garlic 2 min.
  4. Add cold rice, stir-fry 3 min until heated through.
  5. Return chicken + eggs, add soy sauce + sesame oil.
  6. Top with sliced green onions and serve hot.
